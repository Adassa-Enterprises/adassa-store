About Us

The company was established as an Information Technology (IT) company. Currently, our main focus is on the development of desktop, web, server and mobile software applications and IT security.

Applications we develop are usually business automation type software that can be standalone, client-server or enterprise based.

We do not believe in reinventing the wheel so we make extensive use of existing open source software components and frameworks. We make use of ubiquitous object oriented languages such as Java, C++ and Python and all the relevant and related technologies such as web services.

We are constantly expanding our expertise in business and software development so we remain open to requests for products and services that we do not presently offer.
